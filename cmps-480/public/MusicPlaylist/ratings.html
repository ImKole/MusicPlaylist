Playlist Ratings


What Does My Rating Mean?

  • - Ratings are a way to make your playlist experience more tailored for you!
  • - The higher the rating, the higher the song will show up on your playlist.
  • - A 1-Rating will still add your song, but it will appear lower down.
  • - A 5-Rating will add your song to the top of your playlist and will be the first to play when selected.
  • - Songs with the same ratings will appear near the same spot, but will change based on how many times the song has been played.
